Welcome to Our Lab!

We are the BioRAILab, a research group at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. Our work explores bio-inspired sensing, neuromorphic AI, spatial intelligence, and embodied robotics.

This site will serve as our official hub for projects, publications, and open-source software.
